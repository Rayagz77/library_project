FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

"Miel des mots" is an eBook sales platform that offers unique features like an AI-powered text generator that allows users to create personalized books.

To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button on the login page, fill out the required details, and submit the form. You will receive a confirmation email to finalize your registration.

Once logged in, browse our eBook collection, add the desired book to your cart, and proceed to checkout using secure payment options.

The text generator allows you to create a personalized book using AI based on the preferences you select in the form. The book can be up to 10 pages long and is crafted according to your inputs.

You can choose from a variety of genres such as fiction, non-fiction, mystery, fantasy, romance, and more. The AI will generate the content based on your selected genre.

Yes, you can specify the length of your book. The minimum length is 5 pages, and the maximum length is 20 pages.

Simply fill out the Tally form available on the homepage, selecting your preferred genre, themes, and character names. Once submitted, the AI will generate your book accordingly.

If the generated book doesn't meet your expectations, you can request a revision or try creating a new one by adjusting your preferences in the form.

Currently, there is no limit to the number of books you can generate, but each book will be checked for quality and relevance by our admin team before being finalized.

To become an admin, you need to be granted admin rights by an existing admin of the platform. Admins are responsible for overseeing the generated content and ensuring quality standards.

If you forget your password, you can reset it by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions to receive a password reset email.
